Schnitzel


Description

This is how to make a Schnitzel thatâ€™s extra crunchy and
beautifully golden! Schnitzel can be made with pork, veal,
chicken, beef or turkey. So use whatever meat you want.

Ingredients

  • 600g / 1.2 lb large chicken breasts (2 pieces,300g / 10 oz each)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups panko breadcrumbs
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Place the protein of choice on a work surface and cover
    with a sheet of baking paper or cling wrap. Use a rolling
    pin to pound to even thickness about 0.8 cm / 1/3" thick
    (don't pound too thin, hard to handle / might tear).
  2. Place flour in one dish, panko in another (or use
    a large tray for both like I did, see photos), and
    whisk eggs in a bowl.
  3. Heat 1.5cm / 3/5" oil in a heavy based deep skillet
    over medium high heat.
  4. Carefully place 2 schnitzels in the oil and cook for
    3 minutes until deep golden, turn then cook the
    other side for 3 minutes.
